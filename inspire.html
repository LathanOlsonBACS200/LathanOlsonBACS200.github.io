Why is Matthew McConaughey an Inspiration?

Not only is Matthew Mcconaughey an actor in my favorite film of all time, he also has become an author to an inspiring autobiography called Greenlights.

In his remarkable novel McConaughey not only dives into his past, but lessons he wants to share to those willing to accept them. The book is called Greenlights, which is a analogy for "catching more greenlights in a world full of red ones". In this novel the resilience and determination of Matthew in his past give insight into how he has become the inspiration he is today.
